Three American breakfast food we all love

Looking for some sweet breakfast recipes to make your morning extra special?

There's just something about this time of year that has me reaching for my sweet breakfast dishes. You'd think I'd be all sugared out, but the truth is, I just can't get enough. Sure, overnight oats make for a sensible and filling breakfast, and eggs are always recommended. But don't we all go weak in the knees at the sight of French toast? How about a big stack of thick pancakes dripping with syrup?

If breakfast really is the most important meal of the day, shouldn't it also be the tastiest? You'll love these three sweet breakfast recipes to make your morning extra delicious.
