French Toast

by Jane Doe

Wake up to an irresistibly delicious breakfast that's super quick and easy to whip up using a few basic ingredients you likely already have in your kitchen. Fluffy and tender on the inside and perfectly golden-brown on the outside, making French toast is one of our favorite — and most indulgent! — breakfast dishes.

Difficulty: easy

Preparation time: 5 min

Tags: #breakfast #brunch #dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 4 slices of bread

Instructions

  1. Beat egg, vanilla and cinnamon in shallow dish with wire whisk. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
  3. Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides. Serve with ice cream and strawberries, if desired. Voila, easy and tasty French toast!
